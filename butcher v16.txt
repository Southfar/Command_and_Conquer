* = New in v1.6


NOD Units

-*Grenadier:  A sound grenade thrower for the NOD.
-*V6 Raider:  A Cyborg with a 155mm Artillery Cannon.
-*Speed Engineer:  The fastest engineer prototype available.
-Sub Kill APC:  Subterranean APC with hover missiles
-Advanced Weed Eater:  Weed Eater with a tick tank gun
-Made Medic available for NOD use
-Ghost Hunter:  A GDI Ghost Stalker that costs more and has a 90mm as a secondary weapon
-NOD Stinger:  A modified Orca Fighter; primary weapon same as banshee, secondary weapon is the Orca Fighter's Hellfire.

NOD Structures

-*Fixed Repair Site bug which made it not show up in sidebar.
-*NOD Hospital:  A hospital to heal all infantry.
-*Cannon Yard:  Required to build infantry with cannons.
-*Speed Generator:  Needed to build Speed Engineers and other speed units.
-Repair Site:  Much like the GDI Service Depot but costs 800 more because it is hard to come by in NOD infested areas.


GDI Units

-*Haymaker:  A fearsome unit with a cloak and biker missiles.
-*GDI Jacker:  A cheaper Hijacker but also has less power and is a little slower.
-*MSD 193 Unit:  A fearless misleading civilian that is cloakable.
-*Made Mammoth Tank available for use.
-*Survivor:  A cloakable killing machine with a Cyborg Commando Cannon.
-Amp Kill APC:  Amphibious APC with hover missiles
-Made Cyborg available for GDI use
-Kamikaze:  A civilian that explodes upon killing him, hopefully taking out a few things around him
-CC Counter:  A counter to the NOD Cyborg Commando, includes a 90mm as a secondary weapon
-Hoser:  The fastest air unit with two weapons and a build limit of 5.

GDI Structures

-*Power Temple:  Adds an enormous amount of power to your base but is very costly.
-*Cloak Depot:  Building needed for cloak infantry units.
-Yellow Light Posts:  Naturally, this gives light for night battles and is equipped with a Vulcan Cannon.


Added To Both Sides

Units:
-*Jumpjet Fireman:  A Jumpjet Infantry with the Devil's Tongue Fireball Launcher.
-*Rifleman:  An Infantry unit equipped with the M1Carbine Rifle.
-*Laser Infantry:  Costs 1800 and has Laser Turret lasers as primary weapon.
-Bridge Repair:  Engineer that costs less and is weaker; used to repair bridges, but can be used as an engineer also.


Structures:
-Observation Tower:  Standard tower equipped with SAM missiles
